What is your favorite ice cream flavor?

My personal favorite is peanut butter ice cream. The peanut butter ice cream at Coldstone is delicious, but I never see it at Kent Station anymore. I sometimes see it at other locations, and get it almost ever time. As far as toppings go, I really like Reeses or graham crackers. Actually, why not both?
